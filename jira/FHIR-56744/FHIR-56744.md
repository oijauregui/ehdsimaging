# FHIR-56744: Device.status 1..1 forces a current-status snapshot into reconstructed bundles

## Description

Why is device.status 1..1? If it was used for imaging, it was active then. This seems like an unnecessary constraint, and can lead to confusion if the document bundle (and its elements) is reconstructed later.

Proposed resolution:

Don't require status on the device. Makes it difficult in cases where the composition is generated on the fly and includes a snapshot of the device's current status.

Reporter(s): Brian Swinkels, Epic
