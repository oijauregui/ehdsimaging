## Resolution

- Status: Applied
- Resolution: Persuasive with Modification

ImPatient will be dropped from the model. Profiles with reference to ImPatient (ImAdverseEvent ImDiagnosticReport and ImKeyImageDocumentReference) will reference to EuPatient core instead.  The target of Xt-EHR mapping body.orderInformation.supportingInformation.sexForClinicalUse will be remapped to EuPatient.extension[sex-for-clinical-use]
