# FHIR-51295: Relationship between Manifest and MCWG KOS

## Description

As discussed and agreed upon during the MCWG meeting during the IHE-connecthaton in Vienna (June 2025), the manifest specification should be split off into a separate specification.

This specification will be extended with the MCWG KOS specification. Both specifications will be aligned on required data elements. Examples of fields requiring alignment include URL references to viewers, include URL types and frame access.

The FHIR manifest will be limited to only require fields that are also represented by KOS.

The specification will add a specification that indicates lossless mapping from KOS to FHIR and back. Including more detailed specification on population of the FHIR fields (R4 and R5) from the source DICOM information.

It is the intent that this work will be initiated within HL7-EU. After publication of the first version in August 2025, it is the intent that it will be further managed by IHE-RAD.
