# FHIR-51167: Composition: are 8 required sections really needed ?

## Description

(1)

Having 8 required sections seems to me overconstrained.

Suggest to identify the sections that are mandatory and use obligations to mark those required.

This is what has been done so far for the other guides (including MyHealth@EU).

 

(2) the text says to use section.emptyReason to manage the absence of information, but some of the section have 1.. entry (in this case you cannot have the emptyReason)

(3)

some of the mandatory sections include information that may be already in the header, for example ImagingStudy are already provided in the composition event, do we need to repeat them in a imaging Study section ?

The same for orders, are not those provided in the basedOn entry ?

.. and repeated aslo in the DiagnsticReport resources..

having the same information repeated in different places may also create consistency issues
