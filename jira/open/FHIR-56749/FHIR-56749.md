# FHIR-56749: EhrxfImagingDocumentAccessProvider mixes SHALL and SHOULD inconsistently

## Description

The SHALL and SHOULD are inconsistent here. The inconsistency will lead to inconsistent implementations and assumptions across systems that could lead to a non-interoperable network. Unless we expect a client to absolutely need either of these, they should both use SHOULD obligations.

Proposed resolution:

Make them consistently SHOULD.
