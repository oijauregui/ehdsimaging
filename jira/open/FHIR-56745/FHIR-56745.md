# FHIR-56745: Combination procedures involve multiple imaging sets; cardinality should be 0..*

## Description

ServiceRequestOrderEuImagingAccession is 0..1. Should that allow multiple? There may be a combination procedure that involves multiple distinct sets of imaging — such as a nuclear rest/stress test.

Proposed resolution:

Update the cardinality to 0..*.

Reporter(s): Brian Swinkels, Epic
