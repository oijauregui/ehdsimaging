# FHIR-56372: Absence of an Encounter profile in the Imaging Guide

## Description

I have noticed that the HL7 Europe Imaging Report guide does not include a profile for the Encounter resource.

Could you please clarify:

	The reason this profile was left out.




	Whether there are any plans to add an Encounter profile in future releases of the guide.



Thank you for your insight.

## Resolution

- Status: Resolved - No Change
- Resolution: Considered - Question answered

Encounter has not been modelled as it until now we have not seen a definite reason for requiring this.

It is not required in the Xt-EHR logical model. Although adding the information is allowed by the FHIR model, we are not requiring it nor are we specifying how this is done. It can be added and referred to from the different FHIR resources using the xxx.encounter field.

It has been our assumption that hospilization encounter information will be communicated using the hospital discharge report IG.

As such we do not have plans to include it in the report nor will we forbid it.
